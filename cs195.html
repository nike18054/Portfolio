The differences

Cat

There are so many reasons why cats make such good pets. First of all, they don’t demand nearly as much attention as a dog does. You don’t have to walk the cat and you don’t have to deal with it barking at everything that happens to walk by your house.

You can get cats to play with you, if you want, but they’re just as happy to play on their own. There are plenty of toys that appeal to a cat’s hunting instinct. Mind you, that could be an expensive toy that you bought at a store, or it can be paper crumpled up into a ball. They’re not that fussy.

Dog

Nothing beats a long walk with your four legged friend on a fresh, spring morning. Or seeing the joy on their faces when you pick up a ball and they know it’s playtime in the local park! Even relaxing at home feels better in each other’s company.

Studies have found that dog owners have lower blood pressure, lower cholesterol than non dog owners and are less likely to have minor or serious health problems.

The Main Role

The role="main" refers to the main content of a document. This marks the content that is directly related to or expands upon the central topic of the document. Within any document or application, the author should mark no more than one element with the main role.
